# ESG and Financial Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes the relationship between **Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) performance and financial metrics** across multiple companies.

The dashboard helps in understanding how ESG scores relate to company performance by visualizing key indicators such as **revenue, growth rate, profit margin, and carbon emissions**. It provides insights into trends across **regions, industries, and companies**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze financial performance alongside ESG metrics.
* Identify top-performing companies based on revenue.
* Understand regional and industry-level trends.
* Explore relationships between ESG scores and financial outcomes.

---

## 📈 Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **Total Revenue**
* **Average ESG Score**
* **Average Growth Rate**
* **Total Carbon Emissions**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

The dashboard includes the following visual insights:

* **Revenue by Region** – Comparison of company revenue across global regions.
* **Top 10 Companies by Revenue** – Identifies the highest revenue-generating companies.
* **Revenue Trend by Year** – Shows revenue growth over time.
* **Profit Margin by Region** – Highlights profitability differences across regions.
* **ESG Score vs Revenue (Scatter Plot)** – Analyzes the relationship between ESG scores and company revenue.
* **Carbon Emissions by Industry** – Shows emissions distribution across industries.

1. Download the `.pbix` file from the repository.
2. Open it in **Power BI Desktop**.
3. Explore the dashboard and interact with slicers for **Year, Industry, and Score Category**.
